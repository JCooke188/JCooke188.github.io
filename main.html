About Me

I am a doctoral Candidate at the University of Pennsylvania in the Mechanical Engineering and Applied Mechanics Department. My research focus is in computational fluid dynamics, and my advisor is Dr. George Park.

I completed my BS in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Pittsburgh in 2018 and conducted research under Dr. Hessam Babaee., whom I owe a great deal to for introducing me to the world of CFD, and encouraging me to pursue a graduate education.

I grew up just outside Boston, MA, and am an avid Boston sports fan. While growing up I enjoyed playing hockey, football, and baseball, and I still enjoy watching them today. In my free time, I like to go to the gym, cook and go out to eat at new places, play video games with friends, and read/listen to audiobooks. When I can, I also love to travel, and have been lucky enough to visit Cape Town, South Africa, among many other places across the United States.
